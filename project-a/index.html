What we know so far about [ Moon-bug ]? So far these creatures are another variation of moths, like a mix between a moth and firefly because they glow. For some reason they have a mutation that makes them glow only under a full moon. As of now they seem harmless to people.

Guidelines for human interaction with this creature These insects like light, so when you press the mouse a flashlight will appear and the creatures will follow along. Also, the glowing creatures will only appear during a full moon so press “r” to cycle through different phases of the moon, until you reach a full moon.
